Raft of the Medusa

Theodore Géricault - 1819

Théodore Géricault painted The Raft of the Medusa between 1818 and 1819. It is currently displayed at the Louvre Museum. The painting was inspired by a real French naval disaster in 1816.

Starry Night

Van Gogh - 1889

The Starry Night was painted by Vincent van Gogh in 1889 while he was in Saint-Rémy. It reflects his emotional and expressive use of color.
